Question: Why in the lakehouse we prefer using pyspark and in warehouse TSql?

Answer:


LAKEHOUSE:
    Intro:
      - For processing big raw data
      - In lakehouse data is raw, messy, huge and can be of any format (csv, JSON, logs)
      - It needs heavy processing
    Why PySpark:
      - Can handle big data
      - Distributed processing (runs on clusters, parallel execution)
      - Can perform complex transformations (easy to clean, transform and build pipelines)
      - Runs with any data (Structured + Unstructured)

WAREHOUSE:
    Intro:
      - For analyzing clean data
      - Data is cleaned, structured and needs fast querying
    
   Why SQL/T-SQL:
      - Fast querying on cleaned data
      - Easy and readable (Business people understand SQL, easy for dashboards)
      - Structured data only (Relationships, Schemas, Tables)
      - T SQL adds logic (useful for business rules)
